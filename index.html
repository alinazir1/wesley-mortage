STEVE MEDES

President

For more than 20 years, Steve Medes served as the Executive Vice President of the Retail Division of Franklin American Mortgage Company, creating and implementing growth and marketing strategies that expanded the division from $5 to $10 million in sales to over $1 billion. His oversight included all areas of retail mortgage business including sales, operations, technology, compliance and regulation.Following the acquisition of Franklin American by Citizen’s Bank, Steve served as the Executive Vice President of the Mortgage Division of Commonwealth Bank & Trust in Louisville, Kentucky. Now, Steve brings his growth mindset and extensive knowledge of the business to Wesley Mortgage, where he is excited to be able to directly apply those skills in a nimble, grassroots environment driven by a commitment to cutting edge technology and poised for significant growth.
